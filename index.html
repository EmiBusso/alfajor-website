Alfajor - Sandwich cookies

These cookies are delicious in their simplicity: two buttery cookies with a layer of dulce de leche in the middle.

    Ingredients
  • 7 Tbsp butter, softened
  • 2.5 Tbsp powdered sugar
  • 5 Tbsp flour
  • 5 Tbsp corn starch
  • dulce de leche
    Opcional
  • coconut flakes
  • melted chocolate
